Boss

Description

The Boss is a special sandwich, made with love by "El Jefe". He is always cooking for the students in UNLP. His sandwich is a practical way to eat milanesa, but now it is a sandwich of milanesa.

Ingredients

  • Milanesa (Click here to cook milanesa)
  • Bread
  • Tomatoe
  • Mayonnaise
  • lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook your milanesa

  2. Cut your bread by half

  3. Put the mayonnaise and spread it all the way in the bread

  4. Put your milanesa

  5. Put a slice of tomatoe and a lettuce leaf

  6. Enjoy your sandwich, boss!
