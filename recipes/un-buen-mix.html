A Good Mix

Description

Basically, you grab everything you have and make the mix of your dreams. When you are not in the mood of following rules or steps, you can make The Good Mix.

Ingredients

  • Grab some meat
  • Any vegetable you have
  • Something random you find

"Steps"

  1. Put the pot on the burner

  2. Some oil to avoid your food getting stucked

  3. Put your Ingredients into the pot

  4. Wait for them to be cooked

  5. Hope you had some fun!

  6. You can test your creativity!
